    Spiro Farm, wat is dat?

    Spiro Farm is a place full of the right energy located in the heart of Europe. 3.5 hrs from Berlin and 1.5 hrs from Prague. Spirulina is being grown for food. as well as mushrooms, potatoes, corn and pumpkins. Rocket stove workshops. Sourdough bread/pizza workshops etc.

    Camping for stay. Swimming Pool for leisure. Bonfires for making great music and new friends.
